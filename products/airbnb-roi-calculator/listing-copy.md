## Full description

**Stop guessing whether that Airbnb "deal" actually cash flows.**

This spreadsheet does the underwriting for you. Plug in a property's purchase price,
financing terms, and expected nightly rate — get back cap rate, cash-on-cash return, monthly
cash flow, break-even occupancy, and a 5-year projection, instantly.

Built for short-term-rental investors and Airbnb/VRBO hosts who are done doing this math in
their head (or not doing it at all).

**What's inside (4 tabs):**
- **Inputs** — every assumption in one place: purchase price, down payment, mortgage rate,
  ADR, occupancy, cleaning fees, management fee, taxes, insurance, utilities, and more.
- **ROI Dashboard** — your key numbers, calculated live: NOI, cap rate, cash-on-cash return,
  monthly & annual cash flow, break-even occupancy rate, and a 5-year cash flow projection.
- **Monthly Cash Flow** — a 12-month breakdown with an editable seasonality slider, so you can
  model summer peaks vs. winter slow months instead of a flat annual average.
- **Read Me** — a plain-English walkthrough and definitions, so you're never guessing what a
  number means.

**Why this is different from a generic budget template:**
- Every number is a live formula, not a static estimate — change any input and the whole
  model recalculates.
- Break-even occupancy is solved algebraically from your actual cost structure, not eyeballed.
- Works in both Excel and Google Sheets.
- No macros, no add-ons, no subscription. Buy once, use on unlimited properties.

**Who it's for:**
- First-time STR buyers who want a real answer before making an offer
- Hosts evaluating whether to refinance, raise rates, or switch management companies
- Anyone comparing multiple properties and needs an apples-to-apples number

**Not included:** market-specific ADR/occupancy data (use AirDNA, Rabbu, or your own comps —
this tool is the calculator, not the market research) or tax/legal advice.
