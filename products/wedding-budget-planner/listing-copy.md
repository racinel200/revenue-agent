## Full description

**Stop losing track of who's been paid, who hasn't, and whether you're actually on budget.**

Most wedding budget templates are just a list of numbers you update by hand. This one does the
math for you: log every vendor contract and deposit once, and your category-by-category budget
totals — planned vs. actual, over/under, percent of total — update automatically.

**What's inside (3 tabs):**
- **Budget Overview** — set your total wedding budget and a planned amount per category (venue,
  catering, photography, attire, flowers, music, stationery, cake, transportation, rings,
  officiant, favors, and a miscellaneous buffer). Actual Spent, Difference, and % of Total Budget
  are all live formulas pulling from your vendor list — not numbers you re-type.
- **Vendor & Payment Tracker** — one row per vendor: contract total, deposit amount, deposit
  status, due dates, and a Balance Due column calculated automatically (Contract − Deposit).
  Totals row sums every contract, deposit, and outstanding balance at a glance.
- **Read Me** — a plain-English walkthrough so you know exactly where to type your own numbers.

**Why this is different from a generic checklist template:**
- Every rollup is a live formula (SUMIF-driven), not a static estimate — add a vendor row and
  every category total on the Overview tab updates with it.
- Balance-due tracking means no more digging through email threads to remember what you still
  owe a vendor and when.
- Works in both Excel and Google Sheets.
- No macros, no add-ons, no subscription. Buy once, use for the whole planning process.

**Who it's for:**
- Couples planning their own wedding who want one place to track budget and payments
- Maid of honor / best man / parents helping manage the budget on the couple's behalf
- Independent wedding planners who want a lightweight per-client budget tool

**Pre-filled with a realistic example** (14 categories, 13 vendors, $30,000 budget) so you can
see exactly how it works before you clear it out and enter your own numbers.

**Not included:** a guest list / RSVP tracker or day-of timeline — this tool is budget and
vendor payments only.
